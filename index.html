üìò What is Energy-Efficient CPU Scheduling?

Energy-efficient CPU scheduling is a technique where the CPU decides which process to execute and at what speed so that energy consumption is reduced without affecting important tasks.

This is very important in mobile phones, laptops, and embedded systems, where battery life is limited.

‚öôÔ∏è Why CPU Scheduling Affects Energy

CPU power consumption mainly depends on its operating speed:

  • HIGH Speed ‚Üí More voltage ‚Üí High energy usage
  • MEDIUM Speed ‚Üí Balanced energy and performance
  • LOW Speed ‚Üí Less voltage ‚Üí Low energy usage

If the CPU runs at high speed all the time, energy is wasted even when workload is low.

üîã How Our Scheduling Algorithm Saves Energy

  • Important processes are executed first using priority-based scheduling
  • CPU speed is adjusted based on number of remaining processes
  • High speed is used only when workload is high
  • Low speed is used when workload is small
  • This reduces unnecessary CPU power usage

This behavior is commonly seen in smartphones:

  • Gaming / Video call ‚Üí CPU runs fast
  • Reading / Background apps ‚Üí CPU slows down
  • Phone idle ‚Üí CPU enters sleep mode
